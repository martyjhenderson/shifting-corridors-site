# Pathfinder Society at Diversions

Join us for Pathfinder Society games at Diversions in Coralville!

## Details

- **Date:** February 11, 2026
- **Time:** 5:00 PM - 9:30 PM Central Time
- **Location:** Diversions
- **Address:** 119 2nd St #300, Coralville, IA 52241

## Available Scenarios

1. **The Fanciful March of Urawl** (Pathfinder Scenario, Levels 3-6) - Starting at 5:00 PM - [Sign up here](https://www.rpgchronicles.net/session/e1fa3869-96c4-4a17-946f-19e1c786cd76/pregame)
2. **Unforgiving Fire** (Pathfinder Quest, Levels 1-4) - Starting at 5:30 PM - [Sign up here](https://www.rpgchronicles.net/session/38f140a2-3705-4eca-9c54-2ae962f67f9f/pregame)
3. **Port Peril Pub Crawl** (Pathfinder Quest, Levels 1-4) - Starting at 7:00 PM - [Sign up here](https://www.rpgchronicles.net/session/ae28440b-adc2-4fac-b54a-21c97c87ae32/pregame)

## Special Note

The two quests (Unforgiving Fire and Port Peril Pub Crawl) are each 90 minutes long and worth 1 XP each. Players can sign up for both quests or just one!

## Registration

Please register in advance using the links above. Space is limited to 6 players per game, so sign up early!
